Love Machine Learning Data Visualization

Love Machine Learning is a matchmaking service for Georgia Tech students that matched students with their soulmates by looking at their responses to questions about their personality, beliefs, values, and desires in a partner. Our team visualized the data from the students' responses.

Languages used: Javascript, HTML, CSS
Libraries: D3.js
Other Tools: GIT, GitHub, Adobe Illustrator, Microsoft Excel
Period: Feb. 2020 - Present
