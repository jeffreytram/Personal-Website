Music Listening Times

Music Listening Times is an interactive data visualization of my personal monthly music listening habits over the past 2+ years and 50000+ songs!

Languages used: Javascript, HTML, CSS
Libraries: D3.js
APIs: LastFM API
Other Tools: Firebase, GIT, GitHub, Adobe Illustrator
Period: Feb. 2020 - June 2020

Inspiration:

For the Spring 2020 school semester, I took a data visualization course. I thought it would be extremely fun to visualize this large personal dataset I have while being able to practice my skills!
